Listing card — how to show the cheapest source

Same flat (Siddharth Nagar, Goregaon East · NoBroker ₹32,000 cheapest, also on MagicBricks ₹34,500). The whole card opens the cheapest source; chips open their own.

Option 1 — Add a primary source chip

Every source is a chip. The cheapest is marked primary (★, tinted). "Also listed on" covers the rest.
